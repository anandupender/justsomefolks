So who runs this>?

We’re 2 friends who met while castrating cows (we didn’t know what we were getting ourselves into... ask us at the next pop-up) who love playing with food as a reflection of our nuanced identifies. For us, it’s also about the people at the table as much as it is the food being served. we love building communal experiences, not just dishes. Anand (insta handle) does the cooking & visual design, Netta (insta handle) does the logistics & experience design.
